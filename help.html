Help & Support

  • If you encounter any issues feel free to reach us at wandertales@gmail.com. Our experienced team is always ready to assist you!

  • If you need to cancel or modify your booking, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. Depending on the terms of your booking, we may be able to assist you with changes or provide a refund. Please have your booking reference number ready for faster assistance.

  • To find the best travel packages, you can browse our website’s package section or use our search filters to tailor results based on your preferences (e.g., destination, budget, travel dates). You can also contact our travel experts for personalized recommendations based on your interests and budget

  • Your safety is our priority. We partner with trusted service providers who follow strict health and safety protocols. Additionally, we keep our customers informed about any travel advisories or safety measures in place at their destination. If you have specific concerns, please reach out to us for more detailed information.

  • If you have special requests or requirements, please inform us at the time of booking. We will do our best to accommodate your needs, whether it’s related to dietary restrictions, accessibility, or other preferences. It’s important to communicate these requests early to ensure they can be arranged properly.
